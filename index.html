About Us


Hola Folks! We are TeamStellar, a bunch of developers with similar goals. We here at TeamStellar, are highly passionate about exploring emerging technological fronts while at the same time creating a significant impact in the lives of people. Our key memebers include: Siddhant Shitole, Himanshu Gautam, Devanshi Sharma and Divya Bajaj. All four of us are currently pursuing our undergrad degree at The LNM Institute of Technology, Jaipur. We as indiviuals have pretty diverse set of interests and thought processes but we do have a convergence, it being the desire of fabricate best digital experience for the people of the internet.
